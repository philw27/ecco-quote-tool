Our Standards

The standard behind every Ecco job

Accredited engineers, certified work, and an independent compliance walk-off on every single installation.

Standards aren't a slogan — they're a process

Every job booked through Ecco follows the same quality framework, whoever carries it out and wherever you live. Here's what that means in practice.

Accredited trades only

Gas work by Gas Safe registered engineers. Electrical work by NICEIC-registered contractors to BS 7671. Renewables by MCS-certified installers. No exceptions.

Vetting before joining

Accreditations, insurance, references and workmanship are all checked before an installer joins the Ecco network — and monitored continuously after.

Certification on every job

Every installation is certified and notified where required, with your certificates stored in your Ecco account — not lost in a drawer.

Materials that last

We specify quality equipment from established manufacturers with meaningful warranties — because a cheap install that fails early isn't cheap.

✓ The independent Ecco walk-off

Before any job is closed, an Ecco compliance check independently walks the completed installation off against our standards and current UK regulations. It's a second set of expert eyes on every single job — and if it isn't right, it gets put right before sign-off.

If something's not right

Our support team stays accountable after installation. If anything falls short, we manage the resolution — you deal with Ecco, not a disappearing subcontractor.
