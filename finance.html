Finance

Spread the cost, keep the quality

Flexible ways to pay for your home upgrade — including 0% options on selected products.

Spread the cost of your upgrade

A new boiler, rewire or renewable system is a big investment. Ecco offers finance options at checkout so you can spread the cost into manageable monthly payments — with the same fixed price and the same quality standards.

Pay monthly

Choose a term that suits your budget and spread the cost of your installation over time.

0% options available

Interest-free finance is available on selected products and terms — the full cost, split simply.

Fast decision

Apply online in minutes as part of your booking, with an instant decision in most cases.

No hidden fees

Your monthly amount is shown clearly before you commit. What you see is what you pay.

How it works

Get your fixed price as normal, then choose "pay on finance" at checkout. Pick your deposit and term, complete a short application, and if approved your installation is booked exactly the same way as paying upfront.

Finance is subject to status and affordability checks. Terms and conditions apply. Applicants must be 18 or over and a UK resident. Full representative examples and lender details are shown before you apply.
