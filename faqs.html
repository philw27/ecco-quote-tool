FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about fixed prices, installers, certification and more. Can't find what you need? Just get in touch.

Is the price really fixed?
Yes. Your Ecco price includes materials, labour, certification and VAT. Because we ask the right questions upfront, we don't need to revise it later. In the rare case your answers don't match what the engineer finds on the day, we'll always talk to you before anything changes — you'll never be invoiced a surprise.
Do I need a home survey?
For most jobs, no — our question flow captures what's needed. Some complex jobs (like certain rewires or solar installs) may include a pre-install check, which is arranged as part of your booking at no extra cost.
Who actually does the installation?
A vetted, accredited engineer from the Ecco network — Gas Safe for gas, NICEIC-registered for electrical, MCS-certified for renewables. Every installer is checked before joining and monitored continuously.
What is the Ecco walk-off?
Our independent compliance check. When your installation is finished, Ecco walks the completed job off against our standards and current UK regulations before it's signed off as complete. It's a second set of expert eyes on every single job.
What happens to my certificates?
All certification is issued and stored in your Ecco account, so it's there whenever you need it — for insurance, selling your home, or landlord compliance.
Can I pay on finance?
Yes — choose finance at checkout, pick your deposit and term, and apply in minutes. 0% options are available on selected products. Finance is subject to status; terms apply.
What if something goes wrong after the install?
You contact Ecco, and we manage it. Our support team stays accountable after installation — you'll never be left chasing a subcontractor.
What areas do you cover?
We're currently focused on the North West of England, expanding steadily. Enter your postcode in the quote flow and we'll tell you straight away if we cover you.
