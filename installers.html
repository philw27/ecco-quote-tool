Approved Installers

The network behind the name

Vetted, accredited, insured and continuously monitored — meet the standard every Ecco installer is held to.

What "Ecco Approved" means

Not every installer can carry the Ecco name. Our network is deliberately selective — because our fixed prices and independent sign-off only work when the people doing the job are genuinely good at it.

Accreditation checked

Gas Safe, NICEIC, MCS and PAS accreditations verified directly with the schemes — not taken on trust.

Insurance verified

Current public liability and employer's liability insurance confirmed before any job is assigned.

Workmanship reviewed

Recent installations inspected and references taken before joining — and every Ecco job is independently walked off after completion.

Continuously monitored

Customer ratings, walk-off results and re-checks keep standards high. Installers who fall short leave the network.

Are you an installer?

We partner with quality-focused engineers and firms across the North West. If you take pride in your work and want consistent, well-priced jobs without chasing payments, we'd like to hear from you. Email installers@eccohome.co.uk with your accreditations and coverage area.
