How Ecco works

From your first question to the final install and beyond, our team of guides makes upgrading your home simple, clear and stress-free — and every job is independently signed off before it's done.

Step 1

Tell us about your home

Answer a few quick questions about your property and what you need. No home visit, no waiting around — just a simple set of questions you can do from your sofa.

Rico, your planning guide, uses your answers to find the right options.
Step 2

We check everything

Behind the scenes, we run compliance and safety checks against your details — making sure any recommendation meets current UK regulations and is right for your home.

Atlas, our compliance guide, makes sure everything meets the standard.
Step 3

Get your fixed price

You get a genuine fixed price on screen — not an estimate. It includes everything: materials, labour, certification and VAT. No hidden costs, ever.

Dash lines up engineer availability in your area.
Step 4

Book your installation

Choose a date that suits you and book a vetted, accredited engineer. Your installation is carried out to the highest standard, with all waste removed and certificates issued.

Bruno and the team handle your installation from start to finish.
Step 5

Ecco walks the install off

This is what sets us apart. Every completed installation is independently signed off by an Ecco compliance check — not just the installer. We walk the finished job off against our standards before it's ever considered done, so you know it's been verified by someone whose only job is quality.

✓ Independent sign-off on every single job
Atlas carries out the independent Ecco compliance walk-off.
Step 6

We're here after, too

Once you're up and running, your certificates are stored in your Ecco account and our team stays on hand for anything you need — support that continues long after installation.

Otis, your customer care guide, keeps you looked after.
