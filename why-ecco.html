Why Ecco

Why homeowners choose Ecco

Fixed prices, vetted engineers, and every job independently signed off. Here's what makes Ecco different.

Home upgrades, without the guesswork

Getting a boiler, rewire, solar or heat pump quote usually means phone calls, home visits, vague estimates and prices that creep up. Ecco was built to fix that: answer a few questions, get a genuine fixed price, and book a vetted engineer — all from your phone.

💷 Genuine fixed prices

The price you see is the price you pay. Materials, labour, certification and VAT — all included. No surveys that "revise" the quote, no surprise extras.

🛡️ Vetted, accredited engineers

Every installer in our network is checked for accreditations, insurance and workmanship before they carry the Ecco name — and re-checked regularly.

✅ Independently signed off

Every single job is walked off by an Ecco compliance check against our standards before it's considered complete. Not the installer marking their own homework — us.

📱 Everything in one place

Your quote, booking, certificates and support all live in your Ecco account. No chasing paperwork, ever.

The Ecco walk-off

This is what sets us apart. When the install is finished, it isn't done until Ecco says it's done — an independent compliance check verifies the work against current UK standards and signs it off. You get quality that's verified, not assumed.

Built by people who do the work

Ecco isn't a lead-generation site selling your details to the highest bidder. It's built on real installation experience across thousands of homes in the North West — so the questions we ask, the prices we quote and the standards we hold are grounded in how the work actually gets done.
