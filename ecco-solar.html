Most frequently asked questions
How long does solar installation take?
Most domestic solar installations take 1–2 days. We'll erect scaffolding the day before and have your system generating electricity the same day the panels go up.
Will I need planning permission?
In most cases, no. Solar panels on a domestic property are typically covered by permitted development. If you're in a conservation area or have a listed building, there may be additional requirements — we'll advise you.
Can I earn money from my solar panels?
Yes. The Smart Export Guarantee (SEG) pays you for surplus electricity you export back to the grid. We'll set this up for you as part of your installation.
What warranty do the panels come with?
AIKO NEOSTAR panels come with a 25-year manufacturer guarantee and a 30-year performance warranty. The Sunsynk battery has a 10-year warranty. We'll register all warranties for you on installation day.
